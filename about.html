About Food Deli

Our parent company, FOOD DELI Holdings Limited, founded in January 2021 and listed on the Johannesburg in 2024, it is an food company operating in finding the best recipe meals fo our client. With a strong focus on creating innovative meals that cater to the consumer’s everyday needs, Food Deli concentrates on the food, and personal care segments of the market. As a leading supplier of high quality products, the divisions of FOOD DELI specialise in providing the best quality meals across the world Since Food Deli acquired RECIPETIN® in 2018, the two have become a staple in many South African homes. For more information, contact our team on 067 051 3674 Food Deli® has also partnered with local companies in South Africa (Castello and Lurpak) to bring much-loved global recipes to South African meals.
